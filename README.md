# Rune_snatching_game
Catch the rune before it falls

<img width="500" height="300" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5eaf642e-effc-45bc-a62f-49086a4506aa" />

Instructions:
The point of the game is to catch the "gold" rune <img width="80" height="80" alt="rune" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7f288f77-281e-4d97-8747-be6d3917c585" /> and the "double damage" rune <img width="80" height="80" alt="dd" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4501ef85-ac20-4e22-a88c-c8af8b28c35b" />

The "gold" rune gives you 50 gold and the "double damage" rune gives you 100 gold.
If you dont catch them they will remove 1 live each. You start with 10 lives.

However, there are othe runes and traps:

<img width="80" height="80" alt="creep" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9e46ef7e-ef07-4fb4-bed5-abebda38466e" /> - the creep will attack you if you dont avoid it and will remove 1 live

<img width="74" height="74" alt="hex" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bf3930cb-a0b3-4e32-8c6c-5511f87cd83b" /> - the hex will turn you into slow frog

<img width="80" height="80" alt="haste" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8a22edf5-f21b-4948-8141-6f2769a3f519" /> - the haste rune will double your speed

<img width="80" height="80" alt="invisible" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0c1917f4-b397-4ed6-9d00-d5a6d7a69afd" /> - the invisibility rune will turn you invisible (shocker I know...)

<img width="80" height="80" alt="regen" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8ec27607-c751-45c4-8fec-671ba7d246fa" /> - the regen rune will add 3 lives (maximum lives will not exceed 10)

<img width="80" height="80" alt="water" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/deda7623-7f26-481a-a2e9-2789a2305953" /> - the water rune will add 1 live (maximum lives will not exceed 10)

<img width="80" height="80" alt="shield" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e71b1242-79a7-4b98-86dc-8304b6edabce" /> - the shield rune will protect you from one live removal
